What is Circular Economy?

The Circular Economy is transforming how we produce, consume, and think about resources.

  • Reduce: Minimize energy and material use through efficient design
  • Reuse: Extend product lifecycles through sharing and repurposing
  • Recycle: Transform waste into valuable resources
  • Regenerate: Restore natural systems while creating economic value
